Renewable Energy, Water Supply and Sanitation Promotion Centre

REWSSPC is working as Regional Service Center (RSC) of Alternative Energy Promotion Centre (AEPC)/National and Renewable Energy Program (NRREP). We have combine field-based social marketing activities with a centralised ‘one-stop-shop’ at its headquarters in Chandranigarpur, Rautahat.

We support the after-sales service needs of customers seeking technology spares and advice as well as providing advice and marketing RE and WaSH technologies to new customers. Its extension network of social mobilisers throughout the terai districts in which it works is unmatched in the region.

About

The Renewable Energy, Water Supply and Sanitation Promotion Centre (REWSSPC) is a registered company that seeks to promote the dissemination of renewable energy technologies and the sustained functioning of water supply and sanitation infrastructure through private sector engagement.

Renewable Energy Technologies

REWSSPC has been promoting Renewable Energy technology in Terai and hilly region of Nepal with the support of Alternative Energy Promotion Center (AEPC), SNV, ICIMOD, World Bank and Local Government Authorities. In the present provincial structure of Nepal, REWSSPC has been key in facilitating the alternative energy promotion liaising with provincial government and federal government.

Water Supply & Sanitaiton

REWSSPC works closely with local and district level Government agencies for access to safe water and sanitation abiding by the Master Plan of Government of Nepal.REWSSPC has been moderating water supply needs of the community in Terai region and continuously facilitating awareness campaigns for good health and sanitation.
